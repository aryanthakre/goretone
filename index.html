Our Vision

One significant challenge faced by small businesses in Indonesia & rest of the world is the very limited access to financial services. GoRetone wants to change that. The purpose of GoRetone is to make sure that small businesses have a proper platform to boost their business's popularity and profits. Especially during the time of COVID-19 where small businesses are quickly collapsing, it is extremely important to provide resources to support them over the long term. We hope you find GoRetone impactful for your business!
